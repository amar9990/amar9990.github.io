Who are/what is Tribe 44?

We are Kenyan, We are Indian and We are British but before all that we live for fun and great food. Our aim is to share our recipes and have a ball doing it.

In 2017, the President of Kenya, Uhuru Kenyatta, named Kenyans of Asian origin as the 44th tribe of the country. 

The 44th tribe has its origins in the late 19th century when migrations from one beautiful county, the Indian subcontinent, to another beautiful country, Kenya were taking place. 

The Tribe 44 Kitchen was setup to share the unique and undiscovered cuisine that our community enjoys day to day with a mix of Kenyan, Indian and British influences.
